Map 1: This map shows the location of Todd's Place, my favorite breakfast restaurant. This restaurant is shown first because it a great place to start the day. Todd's Place is my favorite breakfast restaurant because the food is really good and very affordable. I usually order a denver omelette with an order of banana split pancakes which is around $14.00.

Map 2: This map shows the location of Tacos Pirekua a mexican restaurant. Tacos Pirekua is my favorite mexican restaurant and is listed second because it is a great place to eat lunch or dinner. Like Todd's Place, I think the food at Tacos PireKua is really good and affordable especially with the portion of food that is given. At Tacos Pirekua I usually order a burrito which is around $8.00.

Map 3: This map shows the location of Izumi Sushi and Grill. Izumi Sushi and Grill is my favorite sushi restaurant and is shown third because it is a great place to eat dinner. The sushi at Izumi Sushi and Grill is very good, though because it is sushi it can be fairly expensive. At Izumi Sushi and Grill it is very easy to spend anywhere from around $20.00 - $30.00 on just myself with happy hour prices. I usually order a rainbow roll and a house special roll and if I am hungry enough for a third roll, I will get whatever else sounds good.
